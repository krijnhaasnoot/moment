Moment

  Timing, together.

  ---
  Moment is an iOS app that helps couples understand fertility timing as a shared part of their relationship. It's designed for partners who want to approach family planning with clarity, communication, and mutual understanding.

  This is not a medical tool. Moment provides information to support conversations between partners, but it doesn't diagnose, predict, or provide medical advice. It's a product built around respect—for your body, your choices, and your relationship.

  Moment is built by the creator of Kinder.

  What Moment is

  - A shared calendar view for understanding fertility phases
  - A tool for conversation and mutual understanding between partners
  - Focused on clarity, not anxiety
  - Built around consent and respect
  - Non-medical and non-prescriptive
  - Private by design

  What Moment is not

  Moment is not a medical device. It does not diagnose fertility issues, predict outcomes, or provide medical advice. It should not be relied upon as a contraceptive method or as a replacement for professional medical guidance.

  If you have questions about your health or fertility, please consult a healthcare provider.

  Privacy & intent

  Moment is designed for couples who choose to share this information with each other. All data is private, and the app emphasizes informed consent between partners. This is not a surveillance tool—it's a shared resource for mutual understanding.

  Status

  Moment is actively developed and in early stages. This repository reflects the current state of the project.
